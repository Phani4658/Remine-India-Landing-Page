About Us

Welcome to Remine India , a recognized startup committed to sustainability through responsible recycling practices. We specialize in recycling electronic waste (e-Waste) and Lithium (Li) Batteries, with a mission to contribute to a greener future by reducing the environmental impact of these materials. Our team is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of recycling, ensuring that hazardous substances are properly handled and disposed of, and valuable resources are recovered for reuse. With our expertise and cutting-edge recycling technologies, we strive to make a positive impact on the environment and create a more sustainable world for generations to come.
